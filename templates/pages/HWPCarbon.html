HWPCarbon

What is HWPCarbon?

HWPCarbon is a web-based carbon accounting tool calculates cumulative carbon stocks and emissions through time for harvested wood products (HWP) using the Tier 3 Production Approach carbon accounting guidelines developed by the Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change (IPCC).

While HWPCarbon is behind an authenticated user login, you can see it live at: https://hwpc-stg.fs2c.usda.gov


Who uses HWPCarbon?

The US Forest Service and several forest growth research groups that utilize carbon storage data.
