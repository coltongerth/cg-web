Fuelcast


What is Fuelcast?

Fuelcast is a map-based resource that leverages Google Earth Engine and Tensorflow to process near real-time weather and remote sensing data, providing in season forecast estimates of the magnitude and timing of annual production and fuel across coterminous U.S. rangelands. Users can access several cutting-edge information products, with detailed reports in development. The tool provides free, near real-time information to rangeland managers, fire specialists, and producers to act on in a timely manner.
